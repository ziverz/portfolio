GameMAN

Converting a vintage telephone into a gaming console.

GameMAN converts your average telephone call into an incredibly fun gaming experience. Rather than picking up the phone to talk to someone, you pick it up as a controller for the super awesome GameMAN console.

This project involved hardware hacking, custom firmware, and reverse engineering telephone signals to turn rotary dial inputs and microphone audio into playable game inputs.
